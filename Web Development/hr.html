Understanding Your Condition

The **common cold** is a contagious respiratory illness caused by influenza viruses. It spreads easily from person to person through droplets when an infected person coughs, sneezes, or talks. Symptoms typically include a runny nose, sore throat, cough, and sometimes fever.

Learn More About the Common Cold

Recommended Next Steps

Suggested Tests: Not typically required for uncomplicated common colds.

Immediate Steps: Monitor your symptoms closely for the next 24 hours. Ensure you get plenty of rest and stay hydrated.

When to See a Doctor: If your symptoms worsen significantly, persist beyond a few days, or if you develop new, severe symptoms like difficulty breathing, please consult a medical professional immediately.
